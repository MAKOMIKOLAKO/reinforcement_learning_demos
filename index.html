Reinforcement Learning, live in your browser

Three agents, trained offline in Python and exported as checkpoints. Scrub the training-progress slider on each demo to watch policies evolve from random noise to competent behavior, running entirely as client-side JavaScript.

DQN · value-only

Snake

A Deep Q-Network learns to estimate action values and greedily picks the best move to survive and grow.

 REINFORCE · policy-only

CartPole

A REINFORCE agent learns a stochastic policy directly, balancing a pole by sampling actions from probabilities.

 PPO · actor-critic

Pendulum

A PPO agent jointly learns a policy and a value function, swinging up and balancing a continuous-control pendulum.

Not sure how these three differ? See a side-by-side comparison of DQN, REINFORCE, and PPO.

Training happens offline; this site only runs forward passes on exported checkpoints.
